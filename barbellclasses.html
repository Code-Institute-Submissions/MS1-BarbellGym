barbell brothers

first of its kind

A progressive men’s weight lifting class where each session we add progressive overload (more reps, weight or sets) to give the body a reason to grow stronger and bigger. A full body training workout with a group of like minded individuals who have the same goal to get better each session. Progressive workouts based around the key movements to build an awesome physique that is strong and functional.

barbell babes

a class for strong women

The female counter part to the Barbell Brothers class. A progressive weight lifting class for Women who want to be strong and look good, using key movements to build a strong, functional body. A group of likeminded individuals who want to learn to lift and to grow stronger, pushing your limits further each session. No matter if you’re new to lifting or been in the weights room for a while, the weights are only going to go up.
